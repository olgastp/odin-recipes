Pancakes

Fluffy, golden breakfast cakes made from a quick batter of flour, eggs, milk, and baking powder — delicious with syrup or fruit.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch of salat
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp melted butter (plus a little for cooking)

How to make

  1. Mix dry: In a bowl, stir together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt
  2. Add wet: Add milk, egg, and melted butter. Mix until just combined (small lumps are okay)
  3. Heat pan: Warm a nonstick pan on medium heat and add a little butter
  4. Pour batter: Use about ¼ cup of batter per pancake
  5. Cook: When bubbles form on top, flip and cook the other side until golden
  6. Serve: Stack them up and enjoy with syrup, fruit, or anything you like!
