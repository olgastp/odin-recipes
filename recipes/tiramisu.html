Tiramisu

A creamy Italian dessert with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cream, topped with cocoa powder.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (240 ml) strong coffee (cooled)
  • 2 tbsp sugar (for coffee, optional)
  • 2 cups (450 g) mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup (240 ml) heavy cream
  • 3 tbsp sugar (for cream)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 pack ladyfinger biscuits (about 200 g)
  • Cocoa powder (for dusting)

How to make

  1. Make coffee: Brew and let it cool. Add a little sugar if you like
  2. Make cream: In a bowl, whip cream, sugar, and vanilla until soft peaks form
  3. Add mascarpone: Gently mix mascarpone into the whipped cream until smooth
  4. Layer:
    • Dip ladyfingers quickly into coffee (don`t soak)
    • Lay them in a dish
    • Spread half of the cream on top
    • Add another layer of dipped ladyfingers
    • Finish with the rest of the cream
  5. Chill: Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours (overnight is best)
  6. Finish: Dust with cocoa powder before serving
